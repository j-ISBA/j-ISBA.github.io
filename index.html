j-ISBA. The junior section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis

j-ISBA, a short for junior-ISBA, is a section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis (ISBA) dedicated to students and early career researchers within 5 years of having completed their degree.

In particular, we aim to:

  1. Promote and provide a forum for early career Bayesian researchers.
  2. Organize conferences, workshops, and sessions at statistical meetings.
  3. Provide academic networking platforms for early career Bayesian researchers.
  4. Stimulate connections with the Bayesian community at large.

Join j-ISBA

Are you a student or an early career researcher within 5 years of having completed your degree? Are you interested in participating in activities to promote your research and learn about the research of your Bayesian peers? Join j-ISBA!

To become a j-ISBA member, subscribe to ISBA here. In the memberships form you will have the possibility to add your one-year membership to j-ISBA (junior ISBA) for just $5 more.

j-ISBA Blog

The j-ISBA Blog is a new initiative aimed at sharing experiences and interesting materials, exchanging ideas about Bayesian research, and providing useful information on conferences, academic calls, and other interesting events happening in our Bayesian world and departments.

If you want to share with us any relevant post on the above topics, send it by e-mail to jisba.section@gmail.com
