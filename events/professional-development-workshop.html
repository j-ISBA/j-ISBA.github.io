Professional Development Workshop: Navigating the Academic Job Market

Conversations with senior hiring committee members and recent job seekers

Sign up for the online workshop here.

Do you plan to go on the job market in the next few years? Have you ever found it challenging to navigate the job seeking process in academia? Do you know what to write in your cover letter and statements? Do you know how to nail an academic job interview?

Worry no more! You can have all your questions answered (and anxiety addressed) in j-ISBAâ€™s professional development workshop series about the academic job market, starting in September 2023.

The workshop series will consist of two panel discussions, one with senior hiring committee members and the second with recent job seekers. Each workshop will be held online and take approximately one hour. All events are FREE and available to all.

The first workshop will be held in two separate sessions by time zones and geographical locations:

1. EU / UK session: September 15th, 10am to 11am BST. Confirmed panelists: Francesco Stingo, Judith Rousseau, Ruth King.

2. North America session: September 27th, 2pm to 3pm EDT. Confirmed panelists: Alexandra Schimdt, Marina Vannucci, Steve MacEachern, Sudipto Benerjee.

You can register for the workshops here. Zoom meeting link and passcode will be shared before the event to all registered participants. Recordings will be made available after the events.

Please reach out the j-ISBA board at jisba.section@gmail.com if you have any questions.
